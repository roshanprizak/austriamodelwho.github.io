Welcome to Austria Model WHO!

Austria Model WHO is an organization based in Krems and Vienna in Austria, focusing on running simulations of the World Health Assembly

  • Strengthen the global health community in Austria and build a network of future global health leaders.
  • Run simulations of the World Health Assembly, a conference conducted at the World Health Organisation every year in Geneva, Switzerland.
  • Discuss health policy and lay foundations for potential collaborations.

Next: April 2023, Krems

Austria Model WHO is a three day conference to be held from 14-16, Friday to Sunday, April 2023 in Krems and Vienna in Austria.

  • Model WHO is inspired from Model United Nations, which is a popular event conducted at various institutions across the world.
  • It is similar to Model UN, except the discussions are debates are focused on health policy.
  • Participants role play as the delegates of member countries and debate within Regional Blocks and in Plenary Sessions.
  • It gives the participants first hand experience in health policy making in a simulated environment.
  • This is a great opportunity for those interested in global health to meet their peers and join the global health community.
